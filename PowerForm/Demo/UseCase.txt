The Business Case: "Global Purchase Request System"Every company needs a way for employees to request software, hardware, or services. This use case requires looking up vendors, categorizing the purchase, adding line items, and tracking statuses.1. Supporting Lists (The Data Sources)Categories: A simple list of departments (e.g., IT, Marketing, Facilities).Sub-Categories: A list of items tied to a parent category (e.g., "Laptops" tied to "IT"). This drives the Cascade feature.Vendors: A directory of approved suppliers with their contact info. This drives the Autocomplete & Column Mapping feature.PO Line Items: A child list for individual items requested. This drives the Child List (1-to-Many) feature.2. The Main List: "Purchase Requests"This is the list your PowerForm will sit on top of.
